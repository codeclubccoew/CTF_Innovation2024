In the year of 1615, upon a serene summer's day, a mysterious figure doth leave a parcel of parchment upon Shakespeare's threshold. 'Tis a script of sorts, wherein all the characters he hath spun are entwined within two acts, yet in a most confounding manner. After diligent perusal of this enigmatic script, Shakespeare doth ponder what ultimate musings did occupy the mind of this unknown benefactor. Assist Shakespeare in deciphering the hidden intent of the clandestine giver.
